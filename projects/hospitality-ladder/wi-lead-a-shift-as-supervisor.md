# WI — Lead a shift as supervisor

The leadership signal for the Supervisor rung: run the **pre-shift briefing**, direct
the team through service, and be the one who resolves the guest issues and keeps the
floor calm under pressure.

- **Done when:** you regularly lead shifts and the team looks to you on the floor.
