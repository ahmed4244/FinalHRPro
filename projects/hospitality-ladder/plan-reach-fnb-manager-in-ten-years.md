# Reach Food & Beverage Manager in ten years

## Goal
Starting on the floor as a Server, earn the **two promotions** — **Shift Supervisor**
by year five, **Food & Beverage / Restaurant Manager** by year ten.

## Shape
Two slices, one per promotion:
- [[scope-promotion-1-shift-supervisor]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/hospitality-shift-supervisor|Shift Supervisor]] by year five.
- [[scope-promotion-2-food-and-beverage-manager]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/food-and-beverage-manager|F&B Manager]] by year ten.

Each scope's work items are the real checklist: the certifications, the training, and
the projects that prove the rung. The whole map is
[[../../knowledge/hospitality-career-ladder|the career-ladder note]].
