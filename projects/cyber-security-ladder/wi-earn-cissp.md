# WI — Earn CISSP

The architecture-tier certification for the Staff/Principal rung — **CISSP** is the
recognised standard for security leadership and architecture. Pair it with **CCSP**
(cloud security) or an advanced offensive cert (**OSCE/OSEP**) for depth.

- **Done when:** CISSP earned (with the required experience endorsement).
