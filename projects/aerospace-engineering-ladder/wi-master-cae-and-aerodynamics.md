# WI — Master CAE tools + aerodynamics/structures

The study behind the licensure: become fluent in **FEA/CFD** (NASTRAN/Patran, ANSYS)
and **CAD** (CATIA/Creo), and go deep on your domain — Anderson's *Fundamentals of
Aerodynamics* and Bruhn's *Analysis & Design of Flight Vehicle Structures*.

- **Done when:** you can independently run and defend the analyses for your components.
