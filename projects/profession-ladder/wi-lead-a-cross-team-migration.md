# WI — Lead a cross-team migration

Lead a large migration or re-architecture that spans several teams — a
monolith-to-services split, a datastore migration, or a cross-org infrastructure
upgrade. The proof that you can drive change across boundaries **without authority**,
by aligning people on the "why".

- **Done when:** the migration is complete across every affected team, with the old
  path retired.
- **Signals:** the "Tech Lead" Staff archetype and sustained multi-team impact
  ([[../../knowledge/promotion-is-operating-at-the-next-level]]).
