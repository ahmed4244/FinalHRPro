# Promotion 1 — Senior Software Engineer (year 5)

The first slice of [[plan-reach-staff-in-ten-years]]: everything needed to earn the
jump from Software Engineer to
[[../../knowledge/senior-software-engineer|Senior Software Engineer]] by year five.

The theme of this rung is **ownership** — moving from finishing tasks to owning a
feature or service end-to-end ([[../../knowledge/the-scope-ladder-task-to-org]]).
The work items below are the checklist: one cornerstone **certification**, the
**study** that backs it, and the **projects** and **leadership** that prove you are
already operating at Senior scope
([[../../knowledge/promotion-is-operating-at-the-next-level]]).

## Work items
- [[wi-earn-aws-solutions-architect-associate]] — the cornerstone cloud cert
- [[wi-study-system-design-and-ddia]] — the study behind the cert
- [[wi-own-a-service-end-to-end]] — the flagship project
- [[wi-lead-a-team-scoped-project]] — lead a multi-part build to launch
- [[wi-write-an-adopted-design-doc]] — an RFC the team adopts
- [[wi-mentor-a-junior-engineer]] — the leadership signal
