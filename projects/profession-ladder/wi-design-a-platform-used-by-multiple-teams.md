# WI — Design a platform used by multiple teams

The force-multiplier project of the Staff rung: design and ship an internal
platform, shared service or framework that **several teams build on** — something
that multiplies other people's output, not just your own. This is the scope jump
from system to org ([[../../knowledge/the-scope-ladder-task-to-org]]).

- **Done when:** two or more teams depend on it in production, with APIs designed to
  be hard to misuse.
- **Signals:** the "Architect" Staff archetype.

Requirement drawn from [[../../knowledge/staff-software-engineer]].
