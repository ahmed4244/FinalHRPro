# WI — Build and lead the team

The manager's real job is the team: **hire, roster, train, and retain** the floor, set
the culture and standards, and grow supervisors under you — the multiplier that makes
every shift good.

- **Done when:** you've built a team that holds standards and retains, and grown a supervisor.
