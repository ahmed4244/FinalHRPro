# WI — Own a multi-quarter technical strategy

Own a technical strategy or roadmap for a whole domain that runs across several
quarters, and drive it to adoption — often by establishing an **org-wide standard**
(API, testing, or observability) that changes how teams build. Setting direction
several teams follow is the defining Staff act.

- **Done when:** the strategy is written, adopted by the domain's teams, and a
  standard from it is in real use.
- **Framed by:** [[wi-read-staff-engineer-and-the-staff-engineers-path]].
