# WI — Earn food-safety + beverage certs

The cornerstone certs for the Supervisor rung: a **food-safety / hygiene**
certificate (ServSafe or Level 2/3 Food Hygiene, HACCP) plus a **beverage** cert —
**WSET Level 2** (wine & spirits) or a barista/mixology qualification.

- **Done when:** the food-safety cert and one beverage cert are in hand.
