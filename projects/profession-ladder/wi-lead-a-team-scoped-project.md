# WI — Lead a team-scoped project to launch

Be the technical lead for a project on your team with several moving parts — take
it from design doc to launch, coordinating the components and unblocking the people
building them. The first real taste of leading, not just building.

- **Done when:** the project has shipped and you drove the technical decisions and
  the code reviews along the way.
- **Pairs with:** [[wi-write-an-adopted-design-doc]] (the doc that starts it).
