# Reach Principal Security Engineer in ten years

## Goal
Starting from a bachelor's degree as a Security Engineer, earn the **two
promotions** — **Senior Security Engineer** by year five, **Staff/Principal** by
year ten.

## Shape
Two slices, one per promotion:
- [[scope-promotion-1-senior-security-engineer]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/senior-security-engineer|Senior]] by year five.
- [[scope-promotion-2-staff-security-engineer]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/staff-security-engineer|Staff/Principal]] by year ten.

Each scope's work items are the real checklist: the certs, the study, and the
projects that prove the rung. The whole map is
[[../../knowledge/cyber-security-career-ladder|the career-ladder note]].
