# WI — Pass the FE exam and earn the PE license

The cornerstone credential for the Senior rung: pass the **FE (Fundamentals of
Engineering)** exam early, then earn the **PE (Professional Engineer)** license once
you have the required experience — it's what lets you sign off analyses.

- **Done when:** FE passed and the PE license is issued.
