# Reach Staff Engineer in ten years

## Goal
Starting from a bachelor's degree as a Software Engineer, earn the **two
promotions** the brief asks for — **Senior** by year five, **Staff/Lead** by year
ten — and give ourselves an app that shows exactly what each rung requires.

## Why now
The decisions are set: the cluster
([[adr-001-choose-the-software-engineer-cluster]]), the ladder model
([[adr-002-model-the-ladder-on-the-big-tech-ic-track]]), the five-year cadence
([[adr-003-a-promotion-every-five-years]]), and where the data lives
([[adr-004-the-ladder-data-lives-in-one-json]]). This plan turns those decisions
into the concrete courses, certs and projects to actually do — and the Svelte view
that displays them.

## Shape
Two slices, one per promotion:
- [[scope-promotion-1-senior-engineer]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/senior-software-engineer|Senior]] by year five.
- [[scope-promotion-2-staff-engineer]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/staff-software-engineer|Staff/Lead]] by year ten.

Each scope's work items are the real checklist: a certification, a book/course, and
the projects that prove the rung. The whole map is
[[../../knowledge/software-engineer-career-ladder|the career-ladder note]]; the app
renders it from [[adr-004-the-ladder-data-lives-in-one-json|one JSON]].
