# WI — Own a service end-to-end

The flagship project of the Senior rung: take one service or major component and
own it **design → build → ship → operate → iterate**, pager included. This is the
scope jump from task to system made concrete
([[../../knowledge/the-scope-ladder-task-to-org]]).

- **Done when:** the service is in production, you carry on-call for it, and the team
  plans around it as dependable.
- **Proves:** you already operate at Senior scope
  ([[../../knowledge/promotion-is-operating-at-the-next-level]]).

Requirement drawn from [[../../knowledge/senior-software-engineer]].
