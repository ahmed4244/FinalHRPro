# Promotion 2 — Staff / Lead Software Engineer (year 10)

The second slice of [[plan-reach-staff-in-ten-years]]: everything needed to earn the
jump from Senior to
[[../../knowledge/staff-software-engineer|Staff / Lead Software Engineer]] by year
ten.

The theme of this rung is **multiplying** — moving from owning a system to setting
direction several teams build against
([[../../knowledge/the-scope-ladder-task-to-org]]). The work items raise the cert to
the professional tier, add the Staff-track reading, and centre on projects with
**cross-team** reach — the sustained, multi-team impact a Staff packet is judged on
([[../../knowledge/promotion-is-operating-at-the-next-level]]).

## Work items
- [[wi-earn-aws-solutions-architect-professional]] — the professional-tier cert
- [[wi-read-staff-engineer-and-the-staff-engineers-path]] — the Staff-track study
- [[wi-design-a-platform-used-by-multiple-teams]] — the force-multiplier project
- [[wi-lead-a-cross-team-migration]] — lead change across several teams
- [[wi-own-a-multi-quarter-technical-strategy]] — set direction for a domain
- [[wi-grow-senior-engineers]] — sponsor others up the ladder
