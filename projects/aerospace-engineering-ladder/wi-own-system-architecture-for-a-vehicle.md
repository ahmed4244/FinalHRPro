# WI — Own the system architecture for a vehicle

The force-multiplier project of the Lead rung: own the **system architecture and
integration** of a vehicle or major subsystem that several teams build to — the
interfaces, budgets (mass, power, thermal), and tradeoffs.

- **Done when:** the architecture is baselined and teams design to your interfaces.
