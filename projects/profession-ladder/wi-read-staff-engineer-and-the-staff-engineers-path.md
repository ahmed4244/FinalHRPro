# WI — Read *Staff Engineer* + *The Staff Engineer's Path*

The Staff rung is as much a change in *how you work* as in what you know, so the
study is career-craft, not just systems. Two books: **Will Larson — _Staff
Engineer_** (the role and its archetypes) and **Tanya Reilly — _The Staff
Engineer's Path_** (the day-to-day operating manual). Pair with depth reading:
*Fundamentals of Software Architecture* and Google's *SRE* book.

- **Done when:** both books read and noted, and you can name which Staff archetype
  (Tech Lead / Architect / Solver / Right Hand) fits your path.
- **Frames:** [[wi-own-a-multi-quarter-technical-strategy]].
