# WI — Grow senior engineers

At Staff you **sponsor**, not just mentor: visibly level up Senior engineers,
argue their promotion cases, and multiply knowledge through talks, written strategy
and a design-review forum or guild. Growing others is counted as your impact.

- **Done when:** you've sponsored at least one engineer toward a promotion and run a
  recurring forum where the org's designs get reviewed.
- **Grows from:** [[wi-mentor-a-junior-engineer]] at the Senior rung.
