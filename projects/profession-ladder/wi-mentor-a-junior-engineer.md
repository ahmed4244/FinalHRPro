# WI — Mentor a junior engineer

The leadership signal for the Senior rung: take a junior engineer through a
shippable feature — reviews, guidance, unblocking — and start running technical
interviews. Senior is the first rung where you are expected to lift others, not just
yourself.

- **Done when:** a mentee has shipped a feature under your guidance, and you're on
  the interview rotation.
- **Grows into:** [[wi-grow-senior-engineers]] at the Staff rung.
