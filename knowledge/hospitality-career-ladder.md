# The Hospitality (F&B) career ladder

The ten-year map for the **Hospitality / Food & Beverage** cluster. You start on the
floor as a **Server / Tea Boy**; the ladder climbs one rung every five years:

| Year | Rung | The jump |
|---|---|---|
| 0 (now) | Server / Tea Boy | you are here — entry F&B service, ~0 yrs |
| +5 | Shift Supervisor / Head Server | run a shift end-to-end |
| +10 | F&B / Restaurant Manager | run the whole outlet and its P&L |

Rung 1 is [[hospitality-shift-supervisor]]; rung 2 is [[food-and-beverage-manager]].

## The axis every rung moves along
The jump is **scope** ([[the-scope-ladder-task-to-org]]): a server works their own
tables; a supervisor owns a shift and the team on it; a manager owns the whole venue
— its people, standards, and profit. As with any promotion, you earn it by
[[promotion-is-operating-at-the-next-level|already operating at the next level]] —
the shifts you ran, the servers you trained, the numbers you moved.

## The two promotions
- [[hospitality-shift-supervisor]] — the first promotion (+5 yrs)
- [[food-and-beverage-manager]] — the second promotion (+10 yrs)
