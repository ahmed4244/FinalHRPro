# A refusal with a clear reason beats a silent failure

A door sign that says *why* you were refused — and how to fix it — turns an
error into an instruction. Exit 2 with one clear line teaches the caller to
send it right next time; a silent failure teaches nothing and gets debugged at
the worst possible hour.
