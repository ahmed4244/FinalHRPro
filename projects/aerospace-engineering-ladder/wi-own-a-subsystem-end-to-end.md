# WI — Own a subsystem end-to-end

The flagship project of the Senior rung: take one subsystem or component — structures,
propulsion, avionics — and own it end to end: design, analysis, test, and
qualification.

- **Done when:** the subsystem is qualified and you own its analyses and sign-off.
