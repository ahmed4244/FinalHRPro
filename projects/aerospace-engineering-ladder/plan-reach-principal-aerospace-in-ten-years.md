# Reach Principal Aerospace Engineer in ten years

## Goal
Starting from a bachelor's degree as an Aerospace Engineer, earn the **two
promotions** — **Senior Aerospace Engineer** by year five, **Lead/Principal** by
year ten.

## Shape
Two slices, one per promotion:
- [[scope-promotion-1-senior-aerospace-engineer]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/senior-aerospace-engineer|Senior]] by year five.
- [[scope-promotion-2-lead-aerospace-engineer]] — the work to become
  [[../../knowledge/lead-aerospace-engineer|Lead/Principal]] by year ten.

Each scope's work items are the real checklist: the licensure, the study, and the
projects that prove the rung. The whole map is
[[../../knowledge/aerospace-engineering-career-ladder|the career-ladder note]].
