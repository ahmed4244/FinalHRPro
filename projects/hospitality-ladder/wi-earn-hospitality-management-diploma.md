# WI — Earn a hospitality-management qualification

The credential for the Manager rung: a **hospitality / F&B management diploma or
certificate** (e.g. a Diploma in Hospitality Management or a food & beverage
management programme), plus **premises / alcohol licensing** where you'll be the
responsible person.

- **Done when:** the management qualification and any required licence are in hand.
