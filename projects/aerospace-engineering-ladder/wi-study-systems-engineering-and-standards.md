# WI — Study systems engineering + standards

The lead-track study: the **INCOSE Systems Engineering Handbook**, and fluency in the
governing standards — **DO-178C** (airborne software), **AS9100** (aerospace quality),
and airworthiness — so you can own system-level tradeoffs.

- **Done when:** you can lead an architecture-level design and its compliance case.
